So long, and well what is up right now, and putting-- putting, and anna, and hello-- father, and she told me it was you, and i must got here a little while ago, it 's good-- for see you-- following all dem years Anna, it 's nice to see so do you, anna villa; anna villa! Yet, What 's that-- Swedish, and i do n't know it; and gee I had an awful trip coming here, and i 'm all in; i had to sit up inside the dirty coach all night-- could not get no sleep hardly-- and then I had a hard job finding this place. I never been of New York before you know and-- You acknowledge you vas awful booty freezing Anna; and ay bet all men see you fall of love with you by aiming; and cut it; and you talk same as they all do. Obviously, Ai n't no harm for your faded speak dat may Anna; no-- course not, as only-- it is funny to understand you again and not recall anything; and you are like-- an alien; as ay s'pose; ay never come home only few times men you vas kit of sweden. You do n't remember dat, no; as but why did n't you never come home them days, and why did not you never come out West to understand me? Ay tank after your mo would die men Ay vas away on voyage it 's best for you you do not never understand me; as [ He sinks down inside the presidency opposite her dejected-- then turns to her-- sadly. ] Ay do n't know Anna why Ay never come home sweden of ole year; ay want come dwelling conclusion of each voyage; ay want see your mo'der your two brow harder before dey vas drowned you men you vas born-- but-- Ay-- do not go. Ay sign on oder ships-- go South America go Australia travel China go each port all over international many times-- but Ay never go aboard ship sail for sweden, men Ay at money for pay passage home as passenger den-- Ay forget and Ay spend all money. men Ay tank again it 's too late, ay do not know why but dat 's may with the majority sailor fall Anna, and dat ole devil sea make dem crazy fools with her dirty tricks; and it 's so.
